# Green Roof

At our company, we are dedicated to using natural and local materials to create energy-efficient and sustainable homes This particular project in Grand Marais features walls that are nearly 15 inches thick, packed with straw, showcasing our commitment to innovative building techniques. Collaborating with skilled engineers and architects, we are excited to push the envelope of sustainable design. All paneling and siding have been sourced from Northern Minnesota, showcasing our passion for supporting local resources.
